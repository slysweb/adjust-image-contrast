NAME

Adjust Image Contrast


SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 132 characters)
--------------------------------------
Click any image on a webpage and adjust its contrast with a slider. Local-only — no upload, no download.


DETAILED DESCRIPTION (paste into Store listing)
-----------------------------------------------
Adjust Image Contrast lets you boost or soften contrast on any image directly on the webpage you’re viewing.

HOW TO USE
1. Open the extension popup and click “Start Selecting”.
2. Hover over images on the page (they highlight with a dashed outline).
3. Click an image to select it.
4. Drag the contrast slider in the floating panel (0%–200%; 100% = original).
5. Click another image to switch, or press Esc to exit.

WHAT YOU GET
• Select any <img> on the page with one click
• Live contrast preview as you move the slider
• Values above 100% increase punch; below 100% soften the look
• Reset to original (100%) or deselect anytime
• Lightweight on-page HUD so you can adjust after the popup closes
• No download feature — changes stay on the page for quick visual tweaks

IDEAL FOR
• Quickly checking how a photo looks with more or less contrast
• Design and content reviews in the browser
• Recovering punch in flat-looking images during review
• Anyone who needs a fast on-page contrast preview

PERMISSIONS
This extension only accesses the active tab so it can highlight images and apply a local CSS contrast filter. Images never leave your device. Nothing is uploaded to a server.

Tips: Use it on regular http/https pages. Special browser pages (chrome://, about:, etc.) are not supported.

Related full online tool (with download): https://www.rockimg.com/adjust-image-contrast.html
